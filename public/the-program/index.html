FAQs

Can I short sell penny stocks?

Evaluation account:
During the evaluation phase you can short-sell any of our 12,000 symbols list which includes many penny stocks. There is no extra fee for “Hard to Borrow”. You just click on the mouse key and short it.

Funded account:
The trading conditions for a funded account may be different from an evaluation account due to the live market conditions and our LPs (for example: Interactive Brokers) terms.

How do I pass the evaluation phase and get a funded account?

You will pass it when you reach a profit of 2 times your max DD. If your max DD is $2100, when you make $4200, you will go into the big leagues and get funded.

Consistency rules:
1. The trader’s best position can not be responsible for more than 30% of the total profit target.
2. All profitable trades must be at least 10 cents per share in profit.
Example: Buy at $50.10, sell at $50.20 (at least)
A trade that will be closed with a total profit of less than 10 cents per share won’t be counted as a profitable trade.

Am I trading CFDs or real shares?

You are trading real shares.

The evaluation account will be conducted on a simulator account (paper money), but will reflect real market conditions with real-time data feed.
The Funded account will be conducted on a live and or simulator account, depending on the fund risk management decisions.

What is the daily loss? (aka RPD)

A daily loss is your maximum trading risk in $ for any given day.
Since you are trading in 1 pool with unlimited buying power, your only parameter for the risk is your max daily loss. You can choose from 3 different daily loss parameters: $700, $1300, and $2000. Every day your daily loss will reset and you will have the full allowance.

What is my maximum drawdown?

Your maximum drawdown is 3 times your daily loss. If your daily loss is $2000, your max DD is $6000

How does the overnight position work?

Swing trading – overnight holding

Swing trading is allowed on all FTNFU’s programs.
A trader can hold an overnight position during the week and the weekend.
At 15:55 ET the max exposure on all accounts will be reduced automatically to fit the overnight exposure:
On the Mini BP $20,000 to $2k
On the Super BP $80,000 to $8k
On the Extra BP $160,000 to $16k
On the Ultimate BP $260,000 to $26k
Once the exposure drops from intraday to overnight exposure, FTNFU will automatically close the remaining shares/positions that exceeded the overnight exposure.
The adjustment for open positions will be according to an equal % for all open positions to fit their market value.

Please be aware:
A stock that is reporting (during earning season) at after-market hours or the next day premarket won’t be available for overnight holdings.
Example:
If you are in a position on AAPL during regular trading hours, and it’s expected to report on the same day at “After-market”, your position will automatically be closed at the end of the day (16:00 ET)

What is a Pump?

A pump is when we increase your daily loss allowance. Therefore you can risk more and make more money. Every time you will have 5 consecutive winning days with at least 3 times your initial daily loss, we will pump up your daily loss and max exposure by X% (depends on the program you chose) For example:
Your daily loss = $700
1st day made a profit of $300
2nd day made a profit of $400
3rd-day made of $500
4th-day made of $400
5th-day made of $500
In total = $2100 (3 times your initial daily loss)
Now your daily loss Increases automatically by 1 step forward to $735.
(This is how real traders work)

What is Dump?

A dump is when we decrease your daily loss allowance. (it will happen only if you had a Pump first) Every time you will have 5 consecutive losing days with at least 2 times your initial daily loss, we will dump down your daily loss one step back. For example:
Your daily loss = $700
You had a risk Pump to $735 (5% increase)
Now you reached a losing streak:
1st-day loss of $200
2nd-day loss of $300
3rd-day loss of $400
4th-day loss of $200
5th-day loss of $300
In total = $1400 (2 times your initial daily loss)
Now your daily loss decreases automatically by 1 step back to $700.
(This is how real traders work)

What is a Reset / Bypass button?

If you hit the daily loss but you can’t wait to go back in to catch the next big trade, you can use the reset button to bypass that daily pause.
You will find the reset / bypass button at the right corner on your trading program dashboard.
For the Mini BP your reset will cost $25
For the Super BP your reset will cost $50
For the Extra BP your reset will cost $100
For the Ultimate BP your reset will cost $250
